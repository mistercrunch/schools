# Lake Schools Family Survey - Form Specification

## Quick Build Guide for Google Forms

Copy these questions into Google Forms. Estimated build time: 10-15 minutes.

---

## Form Settings

- **Title:** Lake Schools Family Planning Survey
- **Description:** (see below)
- **Collect emails:** Yes (automatic with Google Forms)
- **Limit to 1 response:** Yes (can edit response)
- **Show progress bar:** Yes

---

## Form Description (Copy/Paste)

```
This survey helps us understand what families would realistically do if Zephyr Cove Elementary and George Whittell High School were to close.

Your responses will be used to:
• Provide accurate data to the school board
• Build a factual case for keeping our schools open
• Plan for various scenarios

This is NOT anonymous - we need to count actual students, not just opinions. Your information will be kept confidential and used only for planning purposes.

Questions? Contact: [YOUR EMAIL/PHONE]
```

## Section 4: If Schools Close

**Section Description:**
```
If Zephyr Cove Elementary and George Whittell High School were to close, students would need to find alternatives. Please tell us what your family would MOST LIKELY do.

Be honest - accurate data helps everyone, even if your answer isn't "transfer to valley schools."
```

### Q13. If the lake schools closed, what would your family MOST LIKELY do?
- **Type:** Multiple choice
- **Required:** Yes
- **Options:**
  - Transfer to Douglas County valley schools (Gardnerville/Minden) - ~45-70 min bus ride
  - Transfer to South Lake Tahoe schools (California)
  - Homeschool or online school
  - Enroll in private school
  - Relocate / move out of the area
  - Unsure - would depend on circumstances
  - Other

### Q14. If you selected "Other" or "Unsure," please explain:
- **Type:** Long text (paragraph)
- **Required:** No

### Q15. How confident are you in your answer above?
- **Type:** Multiple choice
- **Required:** Yes
- **Options:**
  - Very confident - this is definitely what we would do
  - Somewhat confident - this is likely but could change
  - Not confident - many factors could influence our decision

## Data Analysis Notes

### Key Metrics to Calculate

1. **Total students represented** = Sum of Q8 across all responses
2. **Future students** = Sum of Q12 where applicable
3. **Retention rate** = % selecting "Transfer to valley schools" in Q13
4. **High-confidence retention** = % selecting valley schools AND "Very confident" in Q15
5. **At-risk students** = Students whose families selected anything other than valley transfer

### Suggested Reporting Format

| Outcome | Families | Current Students | Future Students | % of Total |
|---------|----------|------------------|-----------------|------------|
| Valley schools | X | X | X | X% |
| South Lake Tahoe | X | X | X | X% |
